Experience Vancouver

"By Land, Sea and Air We Prosper"

With its scenic views, mild climate, and friendly people, Vancouver is known around the world as both a popular tourist attraction and one of the best places to live.

Vancouver is also one of the most ethnically and linguistically diverse cities in Canada with 52 percent of the population speaking a first language other than English.

Vancouver has hosted many international conferences and events, including the 2010 Winter Olympics and 2010 Winter Paralympics.

Your plant-based, one-stop shop! This market is completely vegan, gluten-free, and organic. Offering a delicious seasonal menu, fresh grocery produce, and various artisanal products.

1025 Commercial Drive

Rickshaw Theatre

The Rickshaw is a known favorite venue by both bands and local music lovers. It boasts state-of-the-art sound and light systems with stellar sight-lines for a venue of its size.

Address:

254 E Hastings St

Why I can't get enough:

This iconic venue hosts some of the most talented local and upcoming indie artists. Big names tour through as well!

Halsa Spa

A relaxation sanctuary located in the heart of Kitsilano. Book a float, massage, or sauna, and finish off your visit in their meditation room or sky-lit relaxation lounge.

Address:

2028 W 4th Ave

Why I can't get enough:

Let all of your stress melt away in this beautiful spa! The friendly staff make sure every visit is nothing less than exceptional.
